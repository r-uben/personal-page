Welcome to my personal webpage!

My name is Reuben Fernandez Fuertes, and I am a 1st year PhD student in Finance at Bocconi University (Milan, Italia). I hold a BSc degree in Mathematics from the Autonomous University of Madrid (2015-2019), an MSc in Teacher Training (2019-2020) from the European University, and an MSc in Mathematical Finance from the University of Manchester (2020-2021)

On this website you can find more information about my career. Briefly speaking, I am interested in the deep conection between Mathematics and Finance (Quantitative Finance, Mathematical Finance) within highly interesting disciplines such as Asset Pricing, Modern Portfolio Theory, Climate Finance, etc.
